# 📖 SELAH | The Deep Insight Bible

**Link:** [https://stephen-semense.github.io/Selah-Bible/](https://stephen-semense.github.io/Selah-Bible/)

**Selah** [סֶלָה] is a modern, distraction-free Bible experience designed for deep meditation and structured study. Beyond just reading, Selah now incorporates a **structured insight engine** that breaks down verses into historical, linguistic, and spiritual components—helping you not just read the Word, but master it.

---

## 🌟 Why Selah? (For the User)

In a world of surface-level reading, Selah provides the tools for a deep dive:
* **The Student of Scripture:** Use the **Key Aspects** panel to see the "why" behind the "what," including the power of naming and original context.
* **The Busy Commuter:** Use **Resume Audio** and **Speed Control (0.5x to 2.0x)** to listen at your own pace.
* **The Visual Learner:** Track your progress with **Dynamic History Rings** and a "Glassmorphic" UI that prioritizes focus.
* **The Night Student:** A soothing **Aurora Dark Mode** ensures late-night reflection doesn't strain your eyes.

---

## ✨ Feature Deep-Dive

### 🧠 New: Structured "Key Aspects" Engine
Inspired by professional study guides, every verse now features an expandable **Deep Insight** panel:
* **Linguistic Breakdown:** Understand terms like *Raqiya* (Expanse) and *Šāmayim* (Sky).
* **The Power of Naming:** See how God designates purpose and authority through titles.
* **Contextual Analysis:** Learn how a specific day or verse fits into the broader "Rhythmic Order" of the Bible.
* **Deep Meaning Summary:** A modern, italicized reflection to help you apply ancient wisdom to today's challenges.

### 🎧 Professional Audio Suite
* **Multi-Layered Speech:** Listen to the scripture text or toggle audio for the **Expanded Explanations** separately.
* **Auto-Advance & Scroll:** The reader highlights the active verse and automatically moves to the next chapter for a truly hands-free experience.
* **Wake Lock Integration:** Your screen won't dim or lock while the Bible is being read aloud to you.

### 🧭 Navigation & Productivity
* **Verse Teleportation:** A dedicated dropdown to "jump" directly to specific verses within long chapters.
* **Chapter Jump Menu:** Quickly skip between chapters without leaving the reader view.
* **Smart Search:** A global search bar that recognizes book names and references instantly.

### ⭕ History & Progress Tracking
The dashboard features **Progress Rings** that save your reading percentage locally. See at a glance how much of a book you have explored and jump back in with one click.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **Tailwind CSS** - For the modern, glass-card interface.
* **FontAwesome 6** - For intuitive, accessible iconography.
* **JavaScript (ES6+)** - For the context engine, audio synthesis, and history logic.
* **Bible-API** - Providing lightning-fast, zero-latency scripture fetching.

## 🚀 One-Click Deployment

Selah is a **Single-File Application**, making it incredibly lightweight and easy to host:

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. Go to **Settings > Pages**.
3. Set the source to the **Main branch** and click **Save**.
4. Your personal sanctuary is live at your GitHub URL.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Speed
Selah is **Serverless**. All history, progress, and settings are stored locally on **your** device (LocalStorage). There are no accounts, no tracking, and no data collection—just a private space for you and the Word.
