# 📖 SELAH | The Personal Sanctuary Bible

LINK: 

**Selah** [סֶלָה] is a modern, distraction-free Bible web experience designed for deep meditation, daily reflection, and audio-visual study. Unlike traditional Bible apps, Selah is built to help **people** not just read the Word, but internalize its core values through an AI-inspired explanation engine.

---

## 🌟 Why Selah? (For the User)

In a world full of noise, Selah provides a quiet digital space. It is designed for:
* **The Busy Commuter:** Use the **Resume Audio** feature and **Speed Control** to listen to scripture on the go.
* **The Deep Thinker:** Every verse features an **Expanded Explanation** that breaks down ancient text into modern, easy-to-understand life lessons.
* **The Goal Setter:** Track your journey through every book with **Visual Progress Rings** that update in real-time.
* **The Night Student:** A soothing **Aurora Dark Mode** ensures your late-night study doesn't strain your eyes.

---

## ✨ Feature Deep-Dive

### 🧠 Smart Explanation Engine
We believe scripture should be accessible. Our custom algorithm generates a multi-part explanation for every verse:
1. **The Core Value:** Identifying the spiritual anchor (e.g., Resilience, Grace, Integrity).
2. **The Deep "Why":** An expanded look at what the verse means in a modern context.
3. **Personal Reflection:** A direct question to help you mirror the verse in your own life.

### 🎧 Professional Audio Suite
* **Native Speech Synthesis:** High-quality reading for all 66 books.
* **Dynamic Controls:** Toggle between 0.8x (for meditation) and 1.5x (for quick review).
* **Auto-Scroll:** The app follows the reader, highlighting the active verse as you listen.

### ⭕ History & Progress Tracking
Never lose your place again. The dashboard features **History Rings** that save your reading percentage locally, allowing you to pick up exactly where you left off with one click.

### ⚡ Technical Excellence
* **Zero Latency:** Scriptural data is fetched dynamically via [bible-api.com](https://bible-api.com).
* **Privacy First:** All history and theme settings are stored on **your** device (LocalStorage). No tracking, no accounts needed.
* **Responsive Design:** Fully optimized for Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.

## 🚀 One-Click Deployment

This project is a single-file application, making it incredibly easy to host for free:

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. Go to **Settings > Pages**.
3. Set the source to the **Main branch** and click **Save**.
4. Your personal Bible sanctuary is now live at `https://your-username.github.io/selah-bible/`
